Our Mission

More than martial arts — a journey of character.

"To inspire individuals through Wushu by fostering discipline, confidence, respect, and lifelong physical and mental well-being."

At Huacheng Elite, children do more than learn techniques — they begin a journey of character development. Through our curriculum and disciplined training, students cultivate resilience, respect, focus, and strong personal values.

Guided by 武德 (Wǔ Dé), the moral code of Wushu, we nurture growth not only as martial artists, but as confident, principled individuals — shaping the body while shaping the mind and heart.

纪律于练，尊重于行，诚信于德，毅力于志，文化于心。

Head Coach

Dr Royce Ang

In martial arts since 1997 and granted the 5th-generation lineage of Jow Ga Kuen. A national-level coach with decades of experience developing athletes across Wushu, Sanda and Lion Dance.

Advisor

Wu Zhaohua

A professional Wushu Taolu athlete representing China with Jiangsu Province. 2016 Asian Championship title in Men's Changquan, with appearances at the World Games, World Wushu Championships and Asian Games — and champion of the "King of the Kings".

Coach

Wang Huaiyu

Former professional Wushu Taolu athlete of Jiangsu Province, holding a 6th Duan rank and National-Level Qigong Athlete status. A graduate of Shanghai University of Sport with extensive experience coaching youth athletes across China.

Ask us anything
What age can my child start?

Our Wushu Tots programme welcomes children from age 4. From there, students progress into Wushu Junior, Wushu Elite, the Competitive Private Group and our specialised classes as they grow.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes. We offer a no-commitment trial class so your child can experience the studio and coaching first-hand. Book through the form below or message us on WhatsApp.

No experience — is that okay?

Absolutely. Most of our students start as complete beginners. Our structured curriculum meets each child at their level and builds from the fundamentals.

How do I book or cancel a class?

You can view the weekly schedule anytime. To book, create an account and log in. Bookings can't be cancelled online — to cancel a class, please contact the studio directly and we'll help you reschedule.

What are your operating hours?

We're open Monday to Sunday, 10am–10pm. Class timings are confirmed on enquiry.

What should my child wear?

For a trial, comfortable sportswear and bare feet or clean indoor shoes are perfect. We'll guide you on attire and equipment once your child enrols.
